# The Jewelry Business

## Research. Heritage. Craftsmanship.

A multilingual analytical center and digital archive dedicated to jewelry manufacturing, craftsmanship, luxury culture, and historical memory.

Founded in 2002 as a specialized jewelry publishing initiative, The Jewelry Business evolved in 2026 into a digital research hub focused on preserving manufacturing heritage, documenting jewelry culture, and building long-term archival value for future generations.

---

## Jewelry Manufacturing
Technologies, craftsmanship, tools, materials, and manufacturing heritage.

## Luxury & Style
Design philosophy, platinum jewelry, visual culture, and collectible aesthetics.

## Intelligence & Heritage
Research, provenance, archives, auction culture, and historical documentation.
